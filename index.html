Sustaining Practice:: Ritual Love Letters

Use the button on the lower right to generate this interactive web zine.

Love letters to and from Black femme thinkers and makers living a “creative life of refusal,” in the words of Alexis Pauline Gumbs. I share these letters with you as reminders and affirmations to help us sustain our creative practice(s) as a ritual of love to ourselves and our communities. This project was built inside Seeda School by alyssa colette. web zine inspired by are.na board please make please make something today bby.

P.S. use your mouse to drag the letters around.
